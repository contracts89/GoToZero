GOAL OF THE GAME:
----------------
THE GOAL IS TO CATCH THE FALLING NUMBERS
THROUGH MATHEMATICAL OPERATIONS WHICH ALSO
FALL LIKE :
ADDITION, SUBTRACTION, MULTIPLICATION
AND DIVISION TO REACH A SCORE OF ZERO
