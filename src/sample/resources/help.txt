GOAL OF THE GAME:
----------------
THE GOAL IS TO CATCH THE FALLING NUMBERS
THROUGH MATHEMATICAL OPERATIONS LIKE :
- ADDITION
- SUBTRACTION
- MULTIPLICATION
AND DIVISION
TO REACH A SCORE OF ZERO
---------------
IMPORTANT!!!
KEEP FROM SYMBOLS (*&^%$) - they can confuse you :)
---------------
CONTROLS :
LEFT AND RIGHT ARROW FOR PLAYER MOVEMENT
P FOR PAUSE
ESCAPE FOR EXIT
---------------
